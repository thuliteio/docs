This guide helps you diagnose and fix common issues with your Thulite Images installation.

## Verify your configuration

Use the [Settings](/images/reference/settings/) and [Parameters](/images/reference/parameters/) reference pages to confirm that Images is configured correctly. For topic-specific guidance, see the guides in the sidebar.

Then use the shortcode reference pages for [img](/images/reference/shortcodes/img/), [picture](/images/reference/shortcodes/picture/), and [figure](/images/reference/shortcodes/figure/) to make sure Images is configured and working properly.

Also check the partial reference pages for [img](/images/reference/partials/img/), [picture](/images/reference/partials/picture/), and [figure](/images/reference/partials/figure/) to confirm they are set up correctly.

## Consult the documentation

If you cannot find an answer in this documentation, visit the [Thulite Docs](/thulite/) for framework-level guidance. Many Images questions can be resolved by understanding how Thulite works under the hood.

## Ask the community

Check for known [Images issues on GitHub](https://github.com/thuliteio/images/issues) or ask for help in [Thulite Discussions](https://github.com/thuliteio/thulite/discussions). The community is active and happy to help.
