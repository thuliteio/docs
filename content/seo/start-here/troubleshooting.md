This guide helps you diagnose and fix common issues with your Thulite SEO installation.

## Verify your configuration

Use the [Settings](/seo/reference/settings/), [Parameters](/seo/reference/parameters/), and [Page Frontmatter](/seo/reference/page-frontmatter/) reference pages to confirm that SEO is configured correctly. For topic-specific guidance, see the guides in the sidebar.

## Consult the documentation

If you cannot find an answer in this documentation, visit the [Thulite Docs](/thulite/) for framework-level guidance. Many SEO questions can be resolved by understanding how Thulite works under the hood.

## Ask the community

Check for known [SEO issues on GitHub](https://github.com/thuliteio/seo/issues), or ask for help in [Thulite Discussions](https://github.com/thuliteio/thulite/discussions). The community is active and happy to help.
